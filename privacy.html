Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to information collected through this website only. Your medical records and protected health information (PHI) collected during the course of treatment are governed separately by Dr. Saladino's HIPAA Notice of Privacy Practices, which you will receive at the time of your first appointment.

Information We Collect

When you visit this website or submit an inquiry through the contact form, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Your name, email address, and phone number
  • The reason for your inquiry as you describe it
  • Technical data such as your browser type, IP address, and pages visited (if analytics tools are in use)

Important Notice About the Contact Form

The contact form on this website is not a secure, HIPAA-compliant channel. Please do not submit sensitive health information, details about your medical history, or any other protected health information through this form. Use it only to request an appointment or ask general questions.

How We Use Your Information

Information submitted through this website is used solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry or appointment request
  • Contact you to schedule or confirm appointments
  • Improve the functionality and content of this website

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

No Doctor-Patient Relationship

Submitting a contact form or inquiry through this website does not establish a doctor-patient relationship. A doctor-patient relationship is only established upon a formal intake process and mutual agreement between you and Dr. Saladino.

HIPAA & Medical Privacy

Once you become a patient, your protected health information is handled in strict accordance with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). You will receive a separate Notice of Privacy Practices at your first appointment, which explains in detail how your medical information is used and protected.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information submitted through this website. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure. We encourage you to avoid sharing sensitive personal or health information through the contact form.

Third-Party Services

This website may use third-party services such as web hosting providers. These providers may have access to technical data (such as server logs) in the course of providing their services. We do not authorize them to use this information for any other purpose.

Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to request that information you have submitted through this website be deleted from our records, to the extent that it has not been incorporated into a medical record or required by law to be retained.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects when it was last revised. Continued use of this website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact our office directly using the information provided on the Contact page.
